About DJ Tracksuit

Find him on the dancefloor with his (self-proclaimed) great dance moves or in the booth, mixing records at listening bars and clubs all around Europe. DJ Tracksuit’s sound travels through the grooves and rhythms of the African diaspora, taking you to dizzying heights of Spiritual Jazz, heavy lows of Dub, Reggae and Deep House, then twists you into a joyous state with Zouk and Disco.
